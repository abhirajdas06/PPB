We Serve The Best Quality Food

The directors of Palm Pavilion Banquet and Restaurant opened the doors to the residents of Lusaka on 22nd February 2022 to accomplish the directors’ passion and dream of managing beautiful, sophisticated and enjoyable events for the residents of Lusaka. The catering menu boasts flavors of the North Indian frontier including a variety of kebabs and curries that will serve to spice up any event.

The Banquet Hall roars with pride to host a variety of events from weddings, engagement parties, birthdays, business conferences and more. We aim at providing a fully facilitated service to clients and ensure a stress-free event – let us manage it for you!
